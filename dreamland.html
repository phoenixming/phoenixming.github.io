Summary

In this action RPG game, players begin in a post-disaster setting with no memory of recent events. As they explore the world, players have the opportunity to switch between a 2D top-down camera view and a sideway 2D platformer view, immersing themselves in the enemy's dream world. Within the dream world, players acquire new skills to overcome their adversaries.
